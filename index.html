Why VAIDYA

Everything your clinic needs,
in one elegant system.

QR‑Based Check‑in
Patients scan a QR at reception – instantly opening WhatsApp for seamless data capture. No forms, no queues.
AI Triage Assistant
A smart bot collects name, age, and chief complaint before the patient even meets the doctor. Data pre‑fills the dashboard.
Digital Token System
Patients receive a live token with queue position. No crowding – they can wait anywhere and get notified.
Auto Voice Calling
When a token is near, VAIDYA makes an AI voice call to the patient, directing them to the right room – hands‑free.
Live AI Transcription
During consultation, VAIDYA listens and transcribes every word, highlighting medicines and dosages in real time.
Instant WhatsApp Reports
A complete, branded PDF prescription is auto‑generated and delivered to the patient’s phone before they leave.
